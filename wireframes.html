5 — Skipped Transactions Loop

Design Notes

  • Progress bars show each envelope's balance as a proportion of the total across all envelopes — not progress toward a target. Rent is widest because it holds the most money.
  • Dock (screens 3, 4, 5) stays pinned at the bottom; the envelope list scrolls above it so you can reach any envelope.
  • Transactions are shown one at a time in the dock. Use "Allocate" to apply the full amount, or "Split" to break it across multiple envelopes.
  • Splits (screen 4) are entered individually — enter an amount, pick an envelope, add. Repeat until the full transaction is covered.
  • Skipped loop (screen 5) is visually distinct: amber banner, amber-tinted dock, amber progress diamonds, and amber-bordered transaction card.
  • "Done" on screen 5 dismisses the skipped loop entirely (any remaining skipped transactions stay for next session).

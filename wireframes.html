5 — Allocating split parts

Design Notes — v2

  • Screen 3 is the default view whenever there are unallocated transactions — the dock is always present, no tap needed to activate it.
  • Swipe left/right on the transaction card to move between unallocated transactions. The dots show your position and loop back to the start.
  • No Skip button — swiping past a transaction and coming back to it later is the natural equivalent of skipping.
  • Split screen is only about dividing the amount into named parts (with optional notes). Envelope selection happens back on the main screen.
  • After splitting, each part enters the dock as its own item (screen 5). The "✂ Split N of M · [Payee]" tag makes the origin clear. The note (e.g., "Weekly shop") becomes the display name for allocation.
  • Progress bars show each envelope's share of total funds — Rent is widest because it holds the most.
